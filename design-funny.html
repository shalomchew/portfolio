Design Funny

2022

art direction

editorial design

digital publication

videography

Based around a studio project "Design Funny" in which a wooden assemblage was produced, a print publication, digital publication and a video were created as a means to document the processes and experience handling the medium of wood.


The very quality of humour present in the project brief was carried over even in the copy, thereby providing opportunities to experiment and play with typesetting.
